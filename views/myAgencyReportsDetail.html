WHAT WAS I ASKED TO DO

Design a centralized, flexible and extensive reporting platform that can meet the needs of our customers across all Vertafore products

At its core it can allow agencies to own their own report templates and distribute to their staff

It has to cover major functions of legacy Vertafore Classic Reports and Vertafore Reporting, so we can sunset the two products

WHAT DID I INHERIT?

Vertafore Classic Reports – based on SAP Crystal Reports

A legacy Vertafore product with an extremely “old fashion” interface from last century, which is not very pretty.

Vertafore Reporting – based on Oracle Business Intelligence Enterprise Edition (OBIEE)

Vertafore Reporting was introduced with the intention of replacing all Classic reports, however it has not provided the level of flexibility that we want to provide to our users. And due to the end of the contract with Oracle, we have to sunset this product soon.

Develop a deep customer empathy. Understand the customer's needs and desires.

"Design Partnership" Program

During the empathize phase, I, together with PM and developer lead, just jumped on the “Design Partner” call and asked them about their agency data analysis requirements, and pros and cons with current legacy Vertafore reporting products:

What reports do you use most frequently?
What do you like about the current reporting tool?
What don’t you like about the current reporting tool?
Are there any data points/fields you wish were on the report that aren’t currently available?
...
"Design Partnership" Program

A target group of users are invited to a weekly “focus group” session so that we can talk with them and validate our product concepts, vision and roadmaps, and also do usability testing and gather feedback.

Provides focus and frames the problem. Fuels brainstorming "how might we statements".

Persona
MARY
Age: 36
Agency Size: A3
Role: Accountant/Financial Analyst
Line of Business: P&C

Based on the findings from previous phase, we defined target persona as "MARY".

Mary started her career as an accountant at her insurance agency. She spent most of her time documenting financial transactions by entering account information. And now she has worked her way up to overseeing the overall agency financial health as a specialized financial analyst.

Now she spends a large portion of her time preparing financial reports. She wants a flexible reporting platform which allows she to create/tailor financial report templates to reuse, and even distribute/share out to other co-workers to ensure everyone is using the same reports.

Whiteboard Brainstorming

Team of 3 (PM, dev lead and I) constantly got together and brainstormed ideas. White board was our powerful tool.

Create & show a physical form to your solution prototype - anything: paper, interactive.

Wireframe Examples

I brought back brainstorming ideas and white board sketches to create detail wireframes and prototypes.

Test + collect feedback through use of rapid interaction cycles. Refine the solution. Measure & repeat.

Test with Design Partners

And again, every week PM, dev lead and I jumped on the "Design Partner" call and validated the design solutions with our customers. Based on the feedback, we continued to refine the solutions and do rapid iteration cycles.
