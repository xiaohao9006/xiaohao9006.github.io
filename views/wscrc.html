WHAT WAS I ASKED TO DO

In general, the redesign of WSCRC website needs to encourage more interaction with visitors, clearly tell the story of what WSCRC does, and be more lively

Members and Public should be able to view membership information on the website before they apply for membership online

The website should provide a convenient, one-stop connection/directory to all of the resources necessary to do business with China

WHAT DID I INHERIT?

Washington State China Relation Council Legacy Website

The look and functionality of the legacy website are so limited and dated. it is too difficult to learn about new membership opportunities, to deliver organization’s information and promote past and upcoming events.

Understand the customer's needs and desires.

Discover with Clients

Before the project kicks off, we sat with Washington State China Relation Council to discuss their pain points of the current website, the reasons for a complete website redesign and their whole vision for the new website.

We also gathered information about who their audiences are and what the desired actions are going to be.

What specific areas of your current site do you feel are successful? Why are they successful?
What shortcomings exist with the current site and what three things would you change on the site today, if you could?
What are the main reasons you are redesigning your site?
What are your primary online business objectives with the site redesign?
What is the main business problem you hope to solve with the site redesign?
...

Provides focus and frames the problem.

Core Functions and Audience

Based on the conversation, we proposed core functions around different level of the audience – non-members and members. We also worked with Client to finalize the complete content sitemap.

Non-Members

Understand the value of our organization
Search for membership information
Become a member online
Search for events
Find information about our activities

Members

Understand the value of our organization
Login to Members Site to rewew membership or see status
Access Member Directory
See information and photos about our activities
Search for events
See their business promoted on the website

Test ideas with clients as fast as possible.

Fast Prototyping and Testing

With limited project time and budget, I figured the best way to carry out the design is to do fast prototyping, and test with clients constantly.

Adobe Experience Design is a great tool. I can easily export my design from Illustrator and import into Adobe XD, then generate and send the prototype link to client, to have them play with it and give me feedback on a daily basis. We also met every Wednesday to discuss any outstanding issues along the road.

Transform design into real live product. Deliver end to end experience.

Transform the Design

With full-stack capability, I was responsible for turning my design into a function beauty website as well. I chose Amazon Web Services as the staging environment so the client can view the live website as I built out component by component.

You can see the live website here at: https://wscrc.org
